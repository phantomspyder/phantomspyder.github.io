---
title: "Everything is a trade-off"
date: 2025-12-23
---
Have you ever found yourself reaching for a familiar solution or perhaps a trendy new one everyone’s talking about?

As you progress in your career, it’s crucial to understand that software engineering, software architecture and systems design revolve around trade-offs. While we’d ideally have unlimited time and resources, reality often presents constraints.  As engineers, our role is to identify a solution that accommodates these constraints and achieves the desired business outcome.
